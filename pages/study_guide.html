Advice on preparing for your Final Exam of CHEM452 (this is not a study guide, you can use our course site as study guide)

1. Use our course site as your study guide. It has all the information of our experiments for this semester.

2. Review the experiments protocols.

3. Get familiar with the principles behind, e.g. what is the principle of protein molecule separation on a SDS-PAGE gel, what about DNA separation on an agarose gel?

Rule 1: You won't see any questions that were not covered in our class.
Rule 2: Any information that we have discussed in the class could be included in the exam.
Rule 3: You may prepare a ONE PAGE study sheet (written on paper, letter size) which you prepare on your own for the exam.
